Artist Statement


Art to me can be in any kind of form. It can be simple art, art that takes hours, art that is on paper, or even on media. Making art and discovering art in all forms has always been an interest of mine since I was young. I like creating pieces of work that other people take an interest in, or knowledge of my work that they think it's good. When I make art it helps me come to a peace within myself because it helps me focus and distracts me from things I am going through mentally. I went from making traditional art, to media recently which I am still getting used to since it was a shift of work style for me. Other than that seeing, discovering, and learning different art I grew a liking into Digital Media Art. Learning Digital Media I was a little nervous about since I have no knowledge of adobe, photoshop, and big editing programs. I am still in the learning process of it, but so far I really enjoy DMA since I can edit and make art like covers. At first I had no idea about what I wanted to do going into this major career wise, but after careful consideration I realized that I wanted to do Digital Media designing for magazine covers like Vogue, Calvin Klein, etc. I want to be able to edit models in magazines that become popular. Since I wish to do this in the future as my career I am working on my editing, photoshop, and digital media skills. I made a few cover pieces in my Intro to Digital Media class, but I am also learning through other classes I take like photography, and I am also learning by myself as I go so I can make pieces off to the side for a better understanding of learning how to use those platforms. I am trying my best to be successful with pursuing this major and taking it further as a career. I do wish to work in the fashion and design industry, and want my work to be recognized since I know this is a competitive industry to be in for work. However, I am very determined I am going to be in this field working and pursuing my work further by taking it global even. I really want to join the DMA program to further my skills in other DMA related classes to make sure I can get the best results for making content like making covers. I want my skills to be recognizable and to be globally recognized even. It would be a long road for that, but I am willing to work hard to make sure I am successful and I can do things such as this for myself. Having this mindset is what makes me an artist and I believe that anyone can do that same with whatever mindset they wish to have about art in whatever form they wish to see it as.
